Info

Turku's IT students' subject associations Asteriski ry, Digit ry, TuKyData ry, DaTe rf and Infå rf organize the 29th Synergia seminar on 1.11.2017 in Turku. The event gathers students to network and listen to speeches on interesting topics of technology and business.

This year's theme is Smart city, Smart learning. The main parts of the event are experts' presentations about current subjects of smart city development as well as fair stands held by partners and guests. Synergia event also includes an evening program and the traditional Lemminkäisenkierros.

Lemminkäisenkierros

The classic board game by Asteriski is an experience you won't forget(or you wish you could...). The game is played at the after party and teams should arrive at least 15 minutes before the game begins. The game is played by 4 teams of 3 people. Participation costs 15€ per team which includes quite a lot of beer and other delicacies!
